## `Code Presenting`
#### `Default Mode`

@snap[south span-100 text-smallcaps text-06]

## `Code Revealing`
#### `Slow Mode` @fa[walking]

@snap[south span-100 text-smallcaps text-06]
`Slow mode first hides all code on your slide. Then lets you step-focus-and-zoom on specific snippets within that code.`
@snapend

---

@snapend
